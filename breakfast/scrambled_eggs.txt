INGREDIENTS:
50 eggs
11 cups milk
1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon salt
25 pinches ground black pepper
1-1/2 cups and 1 tablespoon olive oil

DIRECTIONS:
1. Beat eggs in a large bowl with a whisk. Add milk, salt, and pepper; beat until well mixed.
2. Coat a large skillet with olive oil and heat over medium heat.
3. Pour 1/2 inch of egg mixture into hot skillet; cook and stir until eggs are set, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Repeat 3 until all of the egg mixture is cooked.
