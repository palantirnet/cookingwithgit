INGREDIENTS:
50 eggs
11 cups milk
1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon salt
25 pinches ground black pepper
1-1/2 cups and 1 tablespoon olive oil

DIRECTIONS:
You have scaled this recipe's ingredients to yield a new amount (25). The directions below still refer to the original recipe yield (1).
1. Beat eggs in a bowl with a fork. Add milk, salt, and pepper; beat until well mixed.
2. Coat a small skillet with olive oil and heat over medium heat. Pour egg mixture into hot skillet; cook and stir until eggs are set, 3 to 5 minutes.
